## Stage 1: Traffic

Three tiers of traffic sources, organized by effort level and lead quality. All roads lead to your Skool funnel page.

### Automatic Traffic — Set It and Forget It

**Paid Ads** *(Tool: Meta Ads)*
Facebook/Meta ads driving directly to your Skool funnel page. Video-first creative, retargeting engaged viewers, lookalike audiences.
- Run video-first ads targeting entrepreneurs and agency owners
- Retarget engaged viewers with direct Skool link ad
- Build lookalike audiences from existing Skool members
- Track CPL and CPC weekly, kill underperformers fast

**Skool Discovery** *(Tool: Skool)*
Organic visibility within Skool's built-in search and discovery. Active communities rank higher.
- Optimize community name with searchable keywords
- Pin a high-value welcome post with a clear CTA
- Maintain high member activity (Skool rewards engagement)
- Encourage members to leave reviews

**After Ad Emails** *(Tool: GoHighLevel)*
Email sequences for people who clicked an ad but didn't join. A 5-email drip that brings them back.
- Triggered when someone clicks ad but doesn't join Skool
- 5-email sequence: value, case study, pain point, urgency, final CTA
- Every email links back to Skool Funnel Page
- Add clickers to retargeting pixel list

---

### Immediate Traffic — Daily Hustle

**Facebook 2-Step Post** *(Tool: Facebook)*
Curiosity-driven posts that end with "Comment [KEYWORD] and I'll send you [resource]." Every comment becomes a DM conversation.
- Use Eli Wilde 2-Step framework for the post structure
- End with a keyword CTA that triggers a DM with Skool link
- Post 3-5x per week minimum
- Repurpose top performers weekly

**IG Story / Post** *(Tool: Instagram + ManyChat)*
Instagram content with a keyword trigger. ManyChat auto-replies with a value message and your Skool link.
- Post story with keyword trigger ("DM me LEADS")
- ManyChat auto-replies with value + Skool link
- Follow up manually if no click within 24hrs
- Track open rates and click-throughs in ManyChat

**YouTube Description** *(Tool: YouTube)*
Every video description includes your Skool link in the first 3 lines. Verbal CTA in the video, pinned comment with the link.
- Skool link in first 3 lines of every description
- Verbal CTA: "Link in description to join the free community"
- Pin a comment with the link
- Repurpose clips as Reels/Shorts with same CTA

**Manual DMs** *(Tool: All Platforms)*
Direct outbound messages to warm prospects. 10-20 per day. Use the ARC framework: Acknowledge, Reframe, Close.
- Target warm prospects (engaged, commented, liked your content)
- ARC framework: Acknowledge, Reframe, Close
- Send 10-20 per day minimum
- Never open with "Hey [Name]." Lead with an observation.

---

### Highest Quality Traffic — Relationship-Driven

**Posts In Other Skool Groups** *(Tool: Skool)*
Cross-post valuable content in 5+ Skool communities weekly. Never pitch directly. Let your profile and value do the selling.
- Find 5+ relevant groups (free AND paid)
- Post 2-3x per week per group
- Optimize your Skool profile with a clear CTA and link
- Engage in comments on other people's posts too

**JV Workshops** *(Tool: Zoom + Skool)*
Joint venture workshops with complementary community owners. Teach for 30-45 min, weave your CTA throughout.
- Pitch 2-3 JV partners per month
- Teach a 30-45 min workshop for their members
- Embed value + CTA at multiple points in the presentation
- Follow up with attendees via DM within 24 hours

**Podcasts** *(Tool: Podcast Platforms)*
Guest appearances on niche podcasts. Share signature stories, CTA to join your free community.
- Pitch 3-5 podcasts per month in your niche
- Prepare 2-3 signature stories from your Story Bank
- CTA: "Join my free community at [Skool link]"
- Repurpose clips as short-form content

## Stage 2: Lead

Every traffic channel funnels into one place. This is the hub of the entire system.

### Skool Funnel Page

Your Skool community landing page. Clear promise, social proof, and an opt-in that qualifies AND triggers automations.

**Clear Promise**
Headline that speaks to exactly who it's for and what they'll get. No vague 'join my community' language.

**Social Proof**
Member count, testimonials, client results. Real numbers, real names, real wins.

**Qualifying Opt-In**
3 questions that qualify the lead AND feed data into your automations via Zapier.

**Welcome Video**
Auto-plays after joining. Orients new members, builds connection, sets expectations.

## Stage 3: Nurture

Two parallel paths fire automatically after opt-in. One through GHL (texts + emails), one through Skool DMs.

### Path A: Phone + Email (GoHighLevel)

Automated texts and a 7-day email sequence build trust and surface buyers.

Flow: Opt-In Questions → Zap (Zapier) → Phone + Email GHL → Automated Texts → Low Ticket Offer → Sales Call

### Path A (parallel): 7-Day Email Sequence

Drip value, build trust, and pitch the low-ticket offer over 7 days.

**Day 1–2:** Welcome + your origin story. Then the #1 mistake most entrepreneurs make with lead gen.

**Day 3–4:** Client case study with real results. Then the system explained at a high level.

**Day 5–7:** Soft pitch the low ticket offer. Crush objections. Hard CTA to book a call or buy.

### Path B: Skool Automated DM

Welcome DM inside Skool that transitions into a real conversation.

Flow: Opt-In Questions → Zap (Zapier) → Skool Auto DM → DM Conversation → Weekly Call / Low Ticket

## Stage 4: Conversion

Multiple paths running simultaneously. Every one leads to a sales call.

**Weekly Community Call**
Live weekly group call. 15 min teaching + 30 min hot seat. Follow up with attendees via DM within 2 hours.
- Same day/time every week (consistency builds trust)
- End every call with a clear next-step offer
- Follow up with attendees via DM within 2 hours
- Record and post replay (becomes conversion content)

**Low Ticket Offer**
$47-$297 product that solves ONE problem completely. Buyers get fast-tracked to a sales call.
- Price point: $47-$297
- Solves ONE specific problem completely
- Positions the high-ticket offer as the logical next step
- Buyers are tagged and fast-tracked to sales call

**Conversion Content**
2-3 posts per week inside the community. Case studies, behind-the-scenes, client spotlights. Each with ONE clear CTA.
- Case studies and client win spotlights
- Behind-the-scenes results and process
- Each post has ONE CTA (DM me, grab offer, book call)
- Drives: purchases, DMs, call bookings, and webinar signups

**Webinar**
Monthly or bi-weekly. 45 min teaching, 15 min pitch. Follow-up sequence for attendees AND no-shows.
- Registration via GHL landing page
- Follow-up sequence for both attendees and no-shows
- Replay posted in community (loops back to conversion content)

**Course Upsell Path**
Free course content in Skool classroom. Low ticket upsell embedded in Module 3-4. Buyers get invited to sales call.
- Free content builds trust and demonstrates expertise
- Upsell embedded naturally in the course flow
- After purchase: automated DM checking on progress
- Day 3-5: invite to a "strategy session"

**DM Conversations**
Every conversion mechanism feeds into DM conversations that qualify and book sales calls. ARC framework. Founder-to-founder energy.

**All paths lead to one place → Sales Call**

## Stage 5: Client

Every sales call is designed to close a high-ticket deal. This is the outcome the entire system is built for.

### New High-Ticket Client

Closed through a sales call. Onboarded into the paid community. Generating results, testimonials, and referrals.

**Deal Size: $5,000 – $10,000**

**Onboarding**
GHL automation triggers welcome sequence. Personal DM from you. Orientation call within 48 hours.

**Testimonial (Day 30)**
Ask for a testimonial at the 30-day mark. This feeds directly back into your Conversion Content.

**Referral (Day 60)**
Ask for a referral at 60 days. Warm referrals feed back into your Highest Quality Traffic tier.
