# Pricing Leadership

## A Closer Who Only
### Closes Is Replaceable.

Ian influenced the pricing model, expanded what was sellable, and raised the revenue ceiling for every deal that came after him. That's what a Growth Operator does.

### 01 Removed the 40% Discount

The core offer was being sold at a 40% discount on the website. Ian made the case to the founder to sell at full price. The discount came down. Every sale after that point was worth more — for every closer on the team.

### 02 Interest on Payment Plans

Payment plans were being offered at face value, leaving revenue on the table. Ian introduced interest charges on installments company-wide. A structural change that improved every future transaction.
